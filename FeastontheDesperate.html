Feast on the Desperate - Video

This video is a visual representation of the themes explored in my research paper "Feast on the Desperate: An Analysis of Social Engineering Attacks Exploiting Human Vulnerabilities". It highlights the psychological tactics used by attackers to manipulate individuals and the importance of awareness and education in combating these threats.
